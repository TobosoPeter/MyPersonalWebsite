Words only help you If You Speak Them

This Hand Must Learn Not To Write About Things Like This.

Another Desperate Poem begging to sound happyApril 13,2017
You asked me what is it that I want.

I gave a vague answer not knowing what you meant but even if I did, what would change anyway. I would still be here. Hanging on a tightrope of this razor’s edge. As if cutting is more natural than bathing, now is it?

I’m tired of being like this. Your only go to option when your conscience is so loud. As if these voices in my head wont stop screaming. Your sink of blood and sin. So go ahead, convince me to stay alive and cleanse all your dirty, all your sappy lies and selfishness. Make me drown on my misery so you can swim on your river of happy. Isn’t that what you want? Me choking on your silent glimpses of him. How you tend to open the window sill just so your eyes can meet. You holding out the door just so he can see everything that I lost. Isn’t that what you want? The satisfaction of seeing me break. The pleasure of being the reason why razor sharp scars burn in my body. Tell me, are you happy? am I everything you ever hoped I could be? lost, paranoid, 2 suicide attempts by 15. Are you proud of this creation? This crushed veneer, crumpled paper, this target practice, A collection of your lies. I am tired of being like this. A museum of everything that hasn’t killed me yet.

Tell me, are you satisfied with my care? because if it isn’t for you and for the thoughts of what we could’ve have been. I would gladly end this torment long ago. Instead I chose to make love with pain every night, Slicing inches of skin every single time. Knowing how you promised the whole world to me, But now it seemed like he has it.

I know it sounds crazy to go this far because of “love”. But what do I know about love? When everyone I would take a bullet for ends up holding the gun. What do I know about love? When love is just the synonym of cumming undone. Because as you stood in the middle of the street, drenched in the light of the post lamp and said you wanted me- I believed it in. Because as you dared to hold my hand while walking the entire campus. Proud- I believed in it.

But you really have to ask me what is it that I want? No, I’m not asking for your return. If that is what you want to hear. I just want you to let me be. Let me do the decisions that I want. Let me do this and don’t hold me back, I’m sure you knew the feeling right? Of being held. I could’ve asked for your honesty and beg for you to stop seeing him but I am so tired of everything right now. I know I sound selfish. I know no soul goes through this world painless but a lifetime of asking never gave me enough answers and I am so tired of talking to the ghosts that live in shot glasses.

I am sorry. But please I just want to fall asleep tonight. I just want to wake up in the morning without wanting to die. Just one morning that no voices screaming.

Please Let me do this. Let me be.
